What is The Roundtable Hold

The Roundtable Hold is a place where fellow tarnished can seek guidance from each other, you may discuss about the lore, gameplay elements, ask others for help, and more

all are welcome in The Roundtable Hold

How to use

Start by creating an account by clicking on the create account text.

Creating an account allows you to create your own posts, comments and replies.

An account will also enable liking and disliking other user content

Likes and dislikes helps other tarnished to figure out which content is credible or not

Click on a post in the home page to view the comments and replies other users have created. This also allows you to click on a username to access their account page.
