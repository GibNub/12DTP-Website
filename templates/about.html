What is The Roundtable Hold

The Roundtable Hold is a place where fellow tarnished can seek guidance from each other, you may discuss about the lore, gameplay elements, ask others for help, and more

all are welcome in The Roundtable Hold

How to use

Start by creating an account by clicking on the create account text.

Creating an account allows you to create your own posts, comments and replies.

An account will also enable liking and disliking other user content

Likes and dislikes helps other tarnished to figure out which content is credible or not

Click on a post in the home page to view the comments and replies other users have created. This page also allows you to click on a username to access their account page.

Rules of The Roundtable Hold

Its important that The Roundtable Hold will remains a safe place for tarnished so we ask you to remain civil and present decency when discussing with other tarnished

Do not harrass others

Do not post anything that could offend others, including but not limited to racism, homophobia, and hate speech

Do not dox or witchhunt others

We ask that you follow basic online ettiquete

we ask that posts and comments remain relevant to Elden Ring

By using this site, you agree to the rules and the consequences for breaking them

Thank you for your consideration
